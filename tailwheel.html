SO YOU CAN REACH YOUR GOALS

At Finding Flight, the goal is always to fly in the SuperCub. No matter what, the airplane will end up off the ground at some point. No training experience is complete without flying the airplane. If the airplane stays on the ground the whole time, the training experience is probably not complete. Also, usually we eat lunch.

BASIC TAILWHEEL ENDORSEMENT COURSE

Our basic tailwheel training course provides the training you need to get your tailwheel endorsement. Tailored to your goals and experience level, this course will give you the skills and techniques you need to be a safe and competent tailwheel pilot.

  • 5-10 Hours Dual-Given (On Average)
  • Ground Instruction
  • 3-Points, Wheel Landings, Cross-Wind Landings
  • Grass and Paved Runways

ADVANCED TAILWHEEL COURSE

Take your flying to the next level with our advanced tailwheel training course. This course is designed for pilots with tailwheel experience who are looking to improve their skills. Tackle tougher weather, trickier landings, and backcountry flying with the skills you learn in the course.

  • High Cross-Wind and Tailwind Landings
  • Backcountry Flying
  • Complex Airports
