WHO WE ARE

Finding Flight is a family owned and operated flight services company based out of Aero Valley Airport (52F) in Roanoke, Texas. We strive to help others experience the magic of aviation through high-quality customer experiences, from flight instruction to backcountry courses to A&P services. We are passionate about all things aviation, and have made it our mission to help ignite that same passion in others.
